# 🎬 Joink — 5-Minute Product Video Script

**Live URL:** https://joink-bice.vercel.app/
**Format:** Screen recording of the live app + voiceover. Captions = lower-third on-screen text.
**Pace:** ~150 words/min → lands on ~5:00. Speak ~10% slower than feels natural.

> **Prep:** Log in first. If a live extraction is risky on Wi-Fi, click **"Load demo
> project"** on the dashboard — it seeds 3 pages, one gracefully-failed URL, and a
> cited chat, so every feature is ready to show.

---

## SCENE 1 · The Hook (0:00–0:30)
**On screen:** Landing page; slow scroll through the 3D hero.
**Caption:** *Joink — web research you can cite.*

> "Every day we research on the web — across a dozen tabs that change by the hour. We
> copy, we paste… and a week later we can't remember where anything came from. **Joink**
> fixes that. It turns any public webpage into structured, source-linked research you can
> actually trust."

## SCENE 2 · The Purpose (0:30–1:00)
**On screen:** "How it works" three steps, then the feature cards.
**Caption:** *Extract → Organize → Ask, with citations.*

> "The problem is simple: people need to collect large volumes of fast-changing web
> information in a structured, trustworthy format. Joink does it in three moves — extract
> content from the sites you choose, organize it into clean structured data, and question
> it by text or voice, with every answer backed by clickable sources."

## SCENE 3 · Sign in & Dashboard (1:00–1:25)
**On screen:** Sign in → dashboard with usage cards + projects.
**Caption:** *Your projects, runs, and usage at a glance.*

> "Sign in, and you land on your dashboard — your projects, recent extraction runs, and
> your monthly usage, all in one place. Let's start a new piece of research."

## SCENE 4 · Create a Project + Voice Agent (1:25–2:05)
**On screen:** New Project → click the **mic** on "Fill by voice" → say *"Research web
scraping basics from example dot com."* → fields auto-fill → confirm.
**Caption:** *Voice agent: speak your topic + website.*

> "Here's something special — I don't even have to type. I press the mic and just say what
> I want. Joink's voice agent hears my topic and the website, fills the form for me, and
> lets me confirm before anything runs. Paste up to ten URLs, choose what to capture —
> metadata, headings, text, links — and hit Extract."

## SCENE 5 · Secure Extraction + Graceful Failure (2:05–2:45)
**On screen:** Extraction progress — per-URL statuses; one shows "blocked/failed."
**Caption:** *Responsible scraping. Partial results never lost.*

> "Watch it work — live status for every URL. Joink extracts only public pages, respects
> robots.txt, and blocks private or unsafe addresses — see this one gets safely blocked.
> And crucially, one failed URL never wipes out the others. You always keep what
> succeeded."

## SCENE 6 · Results Workspace (2:45–3:25)
**On screen:** Overview → Pages (open main text) → Headings (filter H2) → Links (internal
filter) → keyword search.
**Caption:** *Structured, filterable, traceable.*

> "This is the heart of Joink — the results workspace. Overview shows the big picture.
> Pages give you the readable text. Headings rebuild each page's outline. Links are
> deduplicated and split into internal and external. Search across everything — and every
> single record carries its source URL, timestamp, and a confidence indicator. That's
> traceability you can defend."

## SCENE 7 · Grounded Chat + Voice (3:25–4:10)
**On screen:** Chat tab → type *"What does ethical scraping involve?"* → answer with
citation cards → click a citation. Then press mic, ask by voice, toggle read-aloud.
**Caption:** *Answers with citations — or an honest "not enough info."*

> "Now I can talk to my research. I ask a question, and Joink answers using only my saved
> extraction — with clickable citations to the exact source. If the content doesn't
> support an answer, it says so, instead of guessing. And I can ask by voice, and even
> have the answer read back to me. This is AI you can trust, because it always shows its
> work."

## SCENE 8 · Export + Explore + Billing (4:10–4:40)
**On screen:** Export JSON, then CSV (upgrade prompt) → Explore tab (News/Popular) →
Pricing → Razorpay Test-Mode checkout flash.
**Caption:** *Export anywhere. Discover. Upgrade securely.*

> "Take it anywhere — export to JSON or CSV, always with sources and timestamps. Discover
> trending stories in Explore and extract them in one click. And when you're ready,
> upgrade with secure Razorpay checkout — every limit enforced on the server, every price
> locked down."

## SCENE 9 · Close (4:40–5:00)
**On screen:** Back to the landing hero / logo.
**Caption:** *Joink — stop copy-pasting the web. Start citing it.*

> "Fast-changing web, captured with structure and proof. Grounded answers, real citations,
> by text or voice. **Joink — stop copy-pasting the web. Start citing it.** Thank you."

---

## 🎥 Recording tips
- **Tool:** Loom, OBS, or your OS screen recorder. 1080p, at the live URL.
- **Audio:** Record narration separately and lay it over, or speak while clicking.
- **Captions:** Add the bold caption lines as on-screen text for polish.
- **Safety net:** "Load demo project" makes every scene demo-ready without a live network.

## One-liners (thumbnail / intro)
- *Joink — web research you can cite.*
- *Stop copy-pasting the web. Start citing it.*
